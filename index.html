THE POWER OF PAIN

Pain teaches patience

Allover the world there are lots of successful people who have endeavored in different fields. They pursued different works, they were born in different countries, they were raised and studied in different schools but, they have something in common regardless their history: they all embraced pain and they are hardworkers.

It is through pain where someone develops patience, it is through where people become humbled first

I have read Many different books written by multiple authors. Many of the folks to day are not willing to ebrace the pain , yet it's the one that allows us to change. Suffering is the only way to the top, pain is the only way to change, obstacles are the only way to success so, you need to love pain. Whatever, physically , mentally ... it does not matter just shush the people and tell them to let you enjoy your pain.

Be the guy who embraces hard work the grind, do not be afraid of sacrificing some blood
